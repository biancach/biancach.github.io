Bianca Champenois

Hello! My name is Bianca. I am a PhD candidate at MIT in the joint Mechanical Engineering and Computational Science/Engineering program. I work with Professor Themistoklis Sapsis in the SAND Lab (Stochastic Analysis and Nonlinear Dynamics) on data-driven fluid mechanics for ocean and climate modeling. I am curious about all things related to machine learning, reduced-order modeling, data assimilation, uncertainty quantification, Bayesian inference, fluid dynamics, numerical methods, and extreme events. In my free time, I like to fix bikes and make cartoons. Here is my resume.
